Volunteering Abroad

Diverbo is an English college in Spain-'A language immersion program with a cultural twist'

I volunteered for 2 summers in Spain with a program called 'Diverbo' which allowed me to meet young people my own age from different countries and cultures who also shared a love of travelling
The aim was to bring young native English speakers from around the world together with Spanish students for them to learn English through simple interaction with us.

Cultural Exchange
